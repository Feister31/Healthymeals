üí° Budget-Friendly Shopping Tips

  • Buy in bulk: Dried beans, lentils, quinoa, rice, oats are all cheaper in bulk
  • Protein staples: Eggs, Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, canned beans, tofu, and tempeh are your affordable protein heroes
  • Frozen veggies: Just as nutritious and more budget-friendly than fresh
  • Meal prep: Cook grains and beans in batches on Sunday to save time during the week
  • Generic brands: Store-brand Greek yogurt, beans, and grains are often half the price of name brands
